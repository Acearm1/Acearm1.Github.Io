About Us

Established in 2004, Allied Computer Engineering is a family-owned design and manufacturing firm driven by a passion for technological advancement. Rooted in a foundation of computer science expertise, we specialize in cutting-edge solutions, including seamless Internet of Things implementation, intuitive Human-Machine Interfaces (HMI) for industrial and consumer controllers, and comprehensive software consulting. Our dedication to innovation and collaborative partnerships ensures that every project reflects our unwavering commitment to quality and client satisfaction. A core principle at Allied is the strategic deployment of services to address critical public needs. Recognizing technology as a powerful catalyst for positive change, we are dedicated to completing impactful pro-bono projects every quarter, ensuring our expertise benefits underserved communities. More than just a business, we are a family committed to contributing to and shaping the future of technology and our community.
